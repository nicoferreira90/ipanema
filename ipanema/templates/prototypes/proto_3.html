Fluent Python,
taught properly.

Ipanema is a study companion to Luciano Ramalho's Fluent Python — the deepest tour of the language's core API ever written. Each of its chapters becomes a module: short lessons to read, then exercises you solve in your own text editor and submit for checking. No videos, no fill-in-the-blanks. Just you, the REPL, and the ideas that make Python Python.

How checking works

01

Read the lesson

Focused readings, ten minutes each. The idiom, the mechanism, and why the language was designed that way.

02

Solve in your editor

Each exercise is a real Python file. Open it in VS Code, vim, whatever — write until it feels Pythonic.

03

Submit & iterate

Ipanema runs your solution against the chapter's checks and tells you exactly what to revisit.
